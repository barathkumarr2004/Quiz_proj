About

This page provides additional information about the Quiz App, including its development, features, and future plans.

Development

The Quiz App was developed using Angular, a popular front-end framework. It is designed to be fast, responsive, and user-friendly.

Features

  • Multiple choice quizzes on various topics
  • Timed quizzes to challenge your speed and accuracy
  • Progress tracking and scoring
  • User authentication for personalized experience

Future Plans

We plan to add more features such as:

  • Leaderboard to compete with other users
  • More quiz categories and questions
  • Enhanced user profiles with achievements and badges
